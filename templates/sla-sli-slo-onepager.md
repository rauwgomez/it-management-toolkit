# SLA / SLI / SLO (one page)

## Definitions
- **SLI:** what we measure (uptime %, response time, etc.)
- **SLO:** the target for that metric
- **SLA:** the commitment plus what happens if the target isn’t met

## Example
### Service
- Customer portal

### SLI
- Uptime %

### SLO
- 99.9% monthly uptime

### SLA
- 99.9% monthly uptime, excluding scheduled maintenance.
- If below target: escalation / service credits (depending on contract).
